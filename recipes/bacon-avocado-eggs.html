Bacon & Avocado Eggs

Category: Egg Based Breakfasts Prep: 5 min Cook: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 Untested

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 rashers bacon
  • 1 avocado
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Cook bacon until crispy.
  2. Prepare eggs as desired.
  3. Slice avocado.
  4. Serve together.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Simple and filling.
