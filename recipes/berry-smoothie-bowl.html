Berry Smoothie Bowl

Category: Smoothies & Bowls Prep: 10 min Cook: 0 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 Untested

Ingredients

  • 1 cup berries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • GF granola

Method

  1. Blend berries and yogurt.
  2. Pour into bowl.
  3. Top with granola and honey.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Refreshing and naturally sweet.
