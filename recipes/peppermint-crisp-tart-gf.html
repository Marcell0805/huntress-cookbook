Peppermint Crisp Tart (GF)

Category: South African — No Bake Prep: 20 min Cook: 0 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 Untested

Ingredients

  • GF biscuits
  • Peppermint Crisp chocolate
  • Cream
  • Caramel Treat

Method

  1. Layer biscuits.
  2. Add caramel mixture.
  3. Add peppermint crisp.
  4. Chill before serving.

Huntress Notes

South African favourite using gluten-free biscuits. GF Adaptation.
