Gluten-Free Pancakes

Category: Warm & Comforting Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 Untested

Ingredients

  • 1 cup GF flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp GF baking powder

Method

  1. Mix ingredients.
  2. Cook pancakes in a pan.
  3. Serve with berries and honey.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Great for special mornings.
