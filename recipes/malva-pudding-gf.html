Malva Pudding (GF)

Category: South African Prep: 20 min Cook: 35 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 8 Untested

Ingredients

  • GF flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Apricot jam

Method

  1. Prepare batter.
  2. Bake until cooked.
  3. Pour warm sauce over pudding.
  4. Serve warm.

Huntress Notes

Classic South African dessert adapted for gluten-free cooking. GF Treat.
