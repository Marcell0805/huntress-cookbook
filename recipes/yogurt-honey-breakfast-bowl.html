Yogurt & Honey Breakfast Bowl

Category: Smoothies & Bowls Prep: 5 min Cook: 0 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 Untested

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • GF granola

Method

  1. Add yogurt to bowl.
  2. Top with fruit.
  3. Add honey and granola.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Quick and customizable.
