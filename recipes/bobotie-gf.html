Bobotie (GF Version)

Category: South African — Traditional Prep: 20 min Cook: 45 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 6 Untested

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 slices GF bread
  • 1 egg
  • Milk
  • Curry powder
  • Turmeric

Method

  1. Prepare mince mixture.
  2. Add soaked GF bread.
  3. Transfer to baking dish.
  4. Top with egg custard.
  5. Bake until golden.

Huntress Notes

Traditional South African bobotie adapted to be gluten-free and onion-free. GF, Traditional Favourite.
