Sweet Potato Breakfast Hash

Category: Warm & Comforting Prep: 15 min Cook: 20 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2 Untested

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes, diced
  • 1 red pepper
  • 4 eggs
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Cook sweet potatoes until tender.
  2. Add peppers and seasoning.
  3. Create wells and crack in eggs.
  4. Cover and cook until eggs set.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Excellent weekend breakfast.
