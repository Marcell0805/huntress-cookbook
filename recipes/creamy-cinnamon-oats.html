Creamy Cinnamon Oats

Category: Warm & Comforting Prep: 5 min Cook: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 Untested

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup certified GF oats
  • 1 cup milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Honey

Method

  1. Simmer oats in milk.
  2. Stir until creamy.
  3. Add cinnamon and honey.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Warm comfort breakfast.
