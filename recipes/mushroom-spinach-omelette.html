Mushroom & Spinach Omelette

Category: Egg Based Breakfasts Prep: 10 min Cook: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 Untested

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 60g mushrooms
  • Handful spinach
  • 30g feta
  • Butter

Method

  1. Cook mushrooms in butter.
  2. Add spinach until wilted.
  3. Cook eggs separately.
  4. Add filling and feta.
  5. Fold and serve.

Huntress Notes

Gluten-free, onion-free. Great source of protein and flavour without onions.
