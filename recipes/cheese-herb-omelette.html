Cheese & Herb Omelette

Category: Egg Based Breakfasts Prep: 5 min Cook: 8 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 🟢 Huntress Approved

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 30 g cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pinch of garlic powder (optional)

Method

  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat until foaming.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture. Let it set slightly around the edges, then gently push cooked edges toward the centre, tilting the pan so uncooked egg flows underneath.
  4. When the top is still slightly runny, sprinkle cheese and herbs over one half.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute until cheese melts.
  6. Slide onto a plate and serve immediately.

Huntress Notes

Light and satisfying without feeling heavy. The herbs add freshness without triggering any IBS symptoms. Perfect with a slice of GF toast on good mornings.

Fox Notes

Keep the heat low — rushing this omelette makes it rubbery. Huntress requested this again the very next morning. A little extra chives never hurt.
